Enter your first name exactly as it appears on your Social Security card. We use your name to verify your identity.

Valid characters include numbers, letters, space(s), period (.), apostrophe (‘), and hyphen (-). The first character must be a letter and the second character must be nonnumeric.

If your name has changed, you must update it with the Social Security Administration (SSA) before you can create an account. For more information, you can call the SSA at 1-800-772-1213 or visit ssa.gov.
